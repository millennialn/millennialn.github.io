WHO ARE WE?

Brief history

Atlehang means 'to succeed' in Sotho. We are founded on principles of dignity and respect for our people. Our founder, Mr Makhetha, has over 20 years in manufacturing headstones. He has a natural talent for designing and crafting beautiful headstones. He started manufacturing headstones in 1997 while working for various tombstone companies until he started this venture in 2013. Today, based in the Vaal Triangle, we have grown to a team of over 8. We believe in the spirit of Ubuntu and to serve you, our client, in those same principles. You can rely on us to help you honor your loved ones with a dignified tombstone.

We deliver tombstones all over South Africa and neighboring countries. These include Lesotho, Eswatini, Botswana, Namibia and Zimbabwe.
